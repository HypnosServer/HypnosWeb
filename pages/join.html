Join Hypnos

Hypnos is a technical server, we expect our members to have at least some technical knowledge of the game and have a good understanding of the different mechanics. The application will be reviewed and voted upon by the server's current members. If you are not notified within five days then your application has been denied.

Please join the discord linked in the community tab and fill out the form below. If you are not in the discord when your application is accepted, we may not reach out to you. We also have a ticket system in place for people to show us their previous builds, within the application channel in our discord. You have a low chance of being accepted without opening a ticket.
